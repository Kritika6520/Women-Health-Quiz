Women's Health Awareness

Today, women are making remarkable contributions across the globe:

Over 50% of women are leading their nations and economies.
70% continue to manage household responsibilities.
80% of teenage and adult girls attend school while managing monthly menstruation.

However, a significant number silently battle a condition called PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome). Around 1 in 5 women face PCOS — a real health challenge that brings:

  • Unexplained weight gain & hormonal imbalance
  • Mood swings, anxiety & depression
  • Irregular periods & fertility issues
  • Fatigue, insomnia & low energy
  • Risk of diabetes & metabolic disorders

Sadly, many people aren't even aware of this syndrome. That’s why we created this short quiz — to spread awareness & spark conversations.
